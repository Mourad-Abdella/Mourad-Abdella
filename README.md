## 📊 Projects

### ✈️ Aviation Crisis — US-Iran Conflict Impact on Global Aviation (2026)
8-page Power BI dashboard analyzing the real-time aviation impact of the 2026 US-Iran conflict. Covers conflict strike mapping, airspace closures, airline financial losses, airport disruptions, flight reroutes, and cancellation timelines across 35 airlines and 35 airports.  
`Power BI` `Python` `pandas` `Kaggle`

---

### 🏠 California Housing Price Prediction
Interactive ML playground where users can train Linear Regression and Decision Tree models on California census data directly in the browser. Achieved R² of 0.556 with Decision Tree vs baseline 0.469.  
`Python` `Scikit-learn` `K-Means` `pandas` `Matplotlib`  
🔗 [Live Demo](https://portfoliomourad.vercel.app)

---

### 🎵 Personal Spotify Data Analysis
Analyzed 5 years of personal streaming history (24,000+ records, 689 hours). Built an interactive dashboard with genre breakdown, mood analysis, skip rates, and year-over-year trends. Upload your own data and explore it live.  
`Python` `pandas` `Plotly` `Seaborn` `Google Colab`  
🔗 [Live Demo](https://portfoliomourad.vercel.app/projects/spotify/)

---

### 🛒 ETL Sales Data Pipeline
End-to-end ETL pipeline processing 100+ retail transactions. Designed a normalized 3-table MySQL schema with PK/FK constraints, then visualized KPIs and sales trends in RStudio.  
`Python` `MySQL` `R` `ggplot2`
